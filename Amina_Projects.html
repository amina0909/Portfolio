Name: Scratch Project

Course: AP Computer Science

Date: September 2019

A partner and I created an interactive story using Scratch. This was one of my first experiences coding, so I found it a little challenging. Furthermore, having to code the story in a way where the audience could interact with it, made it even trickier. However, I soon familiarized myself with scratch and the different variables, and successfully completed the project with my partner. My partner and I worked great together, and implemented both of our ideas into the project. The Scratch Project helped improve my collaboration skills.





Name:MIT App Inventor Project

Course: AP Computer Science

Date: September 2019-October 2019

I created a quiz app called 'Fortune Quizzie' in MIT App Inventor, with a partner.The goal of the project was to create a trivia like game app, that would quiz people with miscellaneous questions. This project was quite difficult as it was both, mine and my partner's, first time using MIT App Inventor. It took us a while to figure out how to code in App Inventor, but eventually we got the hang of it. This project also taught me that you may not always agree with your colleagues or partners,but you have to collaborate and cooperate with them, in order for the project to be successful.
